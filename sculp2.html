About the artwork

This piece is one of César's famous compressions. It fuses metal with vehicles and is characteristic of the artist's works. Through his art, he proposed a new framework for reading objects, by compressing the fundamental elements he modifies their appearance. This compression, a radical and provocative gesture, permitted César to fully establish himself as an artist of the New Realists movement.
